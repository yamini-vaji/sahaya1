Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Will the complaint register's name will be known to others?

A. No, victim name will be kept in secret and anonymous.

Q2. Will the action be taken immediately?

A. Yes, action will be taken immediately against culprit by higher authorities as soon as we analyse the complaint.

Q3. Is any protection given to victim?

A. Yes, protection will be given to the victim by the authorities which makes them feel secure.

Q4. Is any evidence needed to take an action?

A. Yes,either one of the evidences like videos,images,recordings.,etc are needed to support your complaint

Q5. How long does it take to process victim's complaint?

A. It takes about 1-2 days for processing the complaint.
